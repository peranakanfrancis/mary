Luke 11:42-46

Church of The Holy Name of Mary
Oct 13, 2021 2 min read Bible verse
How would you react to such a public and scathing tongue lashing? Jesus does not mince any words. The reality is: we all have “a Pharisee” within us. It naturally comes with our human condition. The challenge for us is to recognize when our internal Pharisee is activated. When we become aware that we are judging another, we then have the opportunity and choice to release our judgment. If we can do this, we not only “release” the person we were prepared to judge, we also release ourselves.
