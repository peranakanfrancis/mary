Mark

Mark 9:38-43, 45, 47-48

Today may be a good day for us to ask ourselves: do we cause any of the “little ones” to stray from Jesus?

Mark 9:30-37

Today I invite you to take some time and ask yourself: How important is power, renown and admiration to me? Do I consciously or unconsciously strive to look good? To have others admire me? Or am I content to be simple, pure and loving like a child?

Mark 8:27-35

Today Jesus asks us: “Who do you say that I am?” Take a few moments and seriously ask yourself: Who do I say Jesus is? Who is he to me? Is he simply a man that lived over 2,000 years ago? Or is Jesus real to me? Ask yourself: How does Jesus and his teaching and example of self-sacrificing love, affect my daily choices and actions? Is the impact significant? Or is it minor?

Mark 7:31-37

What is the impediment we long to be healed of? It may be a spiritual, psychological, physical or relational impediment. How does your impediment affect you, the people in your life and the quality of your life?

Mark 7:1-8, 14-15, 21-23

At times, do we judge or critique others because they are not following the law, be that the law of God or the law of the land?

Mark 9:2-10

Have you ever had the experience of hearing or sensing God saying to you: “You are my beloved.” Today I invite you to sit quietly and allow God to speak those words to you.

Mark 6:30-34

We are called to be like Jesus. We are called to nurture the people in our lives, especially the lost, the forgotten, or the unseen.

Mark 6:7-13

Today Jesus is sending us forth! Will we follow in Jesus’ steps and do as he did? We are called! Will we respond?

Mark 6:1-6

Today make time to give thanks for the many people who love and care about you! In some small way, let them know how grateful you are to have them in your life!

Mark 8:21-43

Your trust in Jesus is in direct proportion to your awareness of who He is and how much He has done for you.
